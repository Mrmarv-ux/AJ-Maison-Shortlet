Frequently Asked

Common Questions

Where is AJ Maison located? +

AJ Maison Shortlet is located in Ikate, Lekki, Lagos — one of the most sought-after neighbourhoods for short-term stays. Close to Lekki Phase 1, Victoria Island, and major business districts.

What is the minimum stay? +

Our minimum stay is 2 nights. We also offer weekly and monthly rates at a discount — reach out via WhatsApp for custom quotes.

Is parking available? +

Yes, secure parking is available within the gated compound. There is a dedicated parking space for one vehicle.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept bank transfers (NGN and USD), card payments, and select mobile payment platforms. Payment details are shared after booking confirmation via WhatsApp.

Is a security deposit required? +

Yes, a refundable caution deposit is collected before check-in and returned within 24 hours of checkout, subject to an apartment inspection.

What are check-in and check-out times? +

Check-in is from 3:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be arranged — contact us via WhatsApp to discuss.

Do you offer airport pickup? +

Yes, we can arrange airport pickup and drop-off from Murtala Muhammed International Airport. Let us know your flight details via WhatsApp and we'll coordinate the transfer.
